BACKGROUND INFORMATION

Isiolo Peace Link (IPL), was formed in 2006 under Ministry of Gender and Social Services. IPL and has been instrumental in articulating Peace and Reconciliations issues. It has resolved various pastoral and urban conflicts, as well as offering auxiliary services in giving alerts, to various actors in peace-building and conflict prevention, and the government law enforcement agencies in and outside the County. The Group uses traditional conflict resolutions mechanism as well as other conventional structures in arbitrating, and negotiations to stop conflicts. Isiolo Peace Link also aims to ensure that Violence Extremism is countered through positive youth engagements and empowerment programs. Isiolo Peace Link is part of a network which also acts as a preventive mechanism within communities. In this sense they form a wider institutionalized conflict early warning and early response system which monitors and reports so that preventive action can be taken. Isiolo Peace Link has proven to be a valuable interface structures between the government and community in ensuring Governance structures adopt and operationalize conflict sensitive approaches that enhance peace security and equitable sharing of resources and responsive to community needs..
